About Us

IIIT Lucknow stands among Top IIITs in our country and aims to be a unique and national level "Apex of Excellence" by enhancing India's Technological strength in Information Technology. Its linkage with industrial partners helps the Institute to place the students in the best of places. The Indian Institute of Information Technology looks for both the academic and personal betterment of the students and we're setting our feet forward in the field of Entrepreneurship and hence we present to you En-Spire.

En-Spire is the annual flagship event of E-Cell IIIT Lucknow which aims to bring together the community of students, industry leaders and investors in the new age of entrepreneurship. It aims to inspire people to transform opportunities into real, sustainable models for themselves and their communities.
